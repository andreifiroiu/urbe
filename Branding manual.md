
This is a comprehensive **Branding Manual** for **Ghes**. It is designed to bridge the gap between your "Reliable Assistant" (data-driven, precise) and your "Spontaneous Friend" (kinetic, local, adventurous).

---

## 1. Brand Identity Checklist: Ghes

### **The Name**

- **Origin:** Derived from the Romanian idiom _"a da ghes"_ (to nudge/urge).
    
- **Pronunciation:** Short, punchy, ending in a hard "s."
    
- **The Hook:** "Orașul îți dă ghes. Tu ce faci diseară?" (The city nudges you. What do you do tonigh?)
    

### **The Personality (The Dual Core)**

1. **The Reliable Assistant:** A high-speed data scraper that "combs" the city 24/7. It is accurate, organized, and never misses a detail.
    
2. **The Spontaneous Friend:** A TikTok-style discovery engine that pushes you out of your comfort zone. It is witty, adventurous, and slightly pushy.
    

### **The "No-Sell" Promise**

- **Positioning:** A "gift to the city."
    
- **Differentiation:** No promoted posts, no ads, no clutter. If it’s in the app, it’s because it’s actually happening and matches your vibe.
    

---

## 2. Visual Identity Standards

### **Logo: The Kinetic "g"**

- **The Symbol:** A lowercase geometric "g" where the tail forms a "bent elbow" or "wedge."
    
- **The Action:** The tail is physically nudging an **Electric Persimmon** dot or location pin.
    
- **The Meaning:** Visualization of momentum. The app is the catalyst; the event is the destination.
    

### **Color Palette**

|**Component**|**Color Name**|**Hex Code**|**Purpose**|
|---|---|---|---|
|**Primary**|Deep Midnight Navy|`#0A1128`|Backgrounds, text, "The Assistant" (Trust).|
|**Accent**|Electric Persimmon|`#FF5733`|Action buttons, the "Nudge," "The Friend" (Energy).|
|**Secondary**|Off-White / Ghost|`#F8F9FA`|Clean whitespace for the "Urban Tech" look.|

### **Typography**

- **Headlines:** Bold, sans-serif, geometric (e.g., _Montserrat_ or _Inter_).
    
- **Body:** Clean, high-readability sans-serif (e.g., _Roboto_ or _Open Sans_).
    

---

## 3. Verbal Identity (Voice & Tone)

- **Language:** Romanian (Primary), English (Expansion).
    
- **Style:** Short sentences. Active verbs. Direct address ("Tu").
    
- **Sample Micro-copy:**
    
    - _Notification:_ "Am găsit un pont de care n-ai auzit. Îți dau ghes?"
        
    - _Empty State:_ "Orașul doarme, dar noi nu. Căutăm următoarea ta ieșire..."
        
    - _Feedback:_ "Te-am citit. Îți plac vinyl-urile. De acum, ești la curent."

## 5. Marketing Pillars for Launch

1. **The "Underground" Pillar:** Use the scrapers to find events that _aren't_ on the big ticket platforms. Market this as "The city's secrets, unlocked."
    
2. **The "Anti-Scroll" Pillar:** Contrast the app with the endless, boring scroll of Facebook Events. "Ghes knows you better than you know your Friday night."
    
3. **The "Local Hero" Pillar:** Since it's a gift to the city, partner with local cafes and bars for "Ghes Approved" stickers near their entrance.
